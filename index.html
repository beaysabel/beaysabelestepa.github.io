An Open Letter to My Best Friend

"Above all, you are beautiful. Please do not ever change. You may change people but do not let people change you. Do not let others think less about you because you are already more of us combined. I hope you know these because you deserve the world. You deserve the best. I love you so much. "

An Open Letter To The Girl Who Deserves Better

"Wake up. You do not need them. You do not need him. You deserve someone who will love and appreciate you as you are. You deserve someone who will be with you along your walk through life. It is time for you to move onto the new chapter of your story. It is time for you to be happily writing and cherishing every moment on that new chapter. You must believe that you deserve the best."

About the Author

Bea Ysabel G. Estepa is a student in De La Salle University where she is currently working on getting her degree on Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering. She loves reading books, hanging out with friends, and admiring her unicorn collection.

After taking Industrial Engineering, she is planning to study Bachelor of Science in Marine Biology. She is fond with the adventures and discoveries of scientists about the ocean. She enjoys learning new things about the ocean and what is underneath it.

Aside from that, she also enjoys writing poems and compositions. She finds literature to be something with deep meaning. Furthermore, she is amazed with how literature can express your feelings with such depth.
